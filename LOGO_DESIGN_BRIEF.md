# Quotla Logo Design Brief

## Brand Overview
**Name:** Quotla
**Tagline:** "Your Business Made Simple"
**Industry:** Business management SaaS platform
**Target Audience:** Freelancers, small businesses, and agencies managing quotes and invoices

## Brand Essence
Quotla is an AI-powered business management platform that simplifies quote and invoice creation through natural conversation. The brand represents professionalism, simplicity, intelligence, and trustworthiness. It transforms complex business paperwork into effortless conversations.

## Logo Requirements

### Primary Concept
Create a modern, professional logo that conveys:
- **Intelligence & AI**: The platform uses AI to automate business tasks
- **Simplicity**: Making complex processes simple and accessible
- **Professionalism**: Trusted by businesses for client-facing documents
- **Efficiency**: Streamlined workflow and automation

### Brand Personality
- Professional yet approachable
- Modern and tech-forward
- Clean and sophisticated
- Reliable and trustworthy

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Primary Navy:** `#1e40af` - Main brand color (professional, trustworthy)
- **Primary Blue:** `#3b82f6` - Secondary brand color (modern, accessible)
- **Primary Light:** `#60a5fa` - Accent highlights

### Supporting Colors
- **Dark Navy:** `#1e293b` - Professional depth
- **Lightest Blue:** `#dbeafe` - Subtle backgrounds
- **White:** `#FFFFFF` - Clean, minimalist
- **Text Dark:** `#0f172a` - Primary text

### Gradients (Optional Use)
- Primary gradient: `linear-gradient(135deg, #1e40af 0%, #3b82f6 100%)`
- Dark gradient: `linear-gradient(135deg, #0f172a 0%, #1e293b 100%)`

## Typography
**Font Family:** Poppins (sans-serif)
- Clean, modern, geometric sans-serif
- Professional yet friendly
- Excellent readability at all sizes

## Design Direction

### Mark/Symbol Concepts
Consider incorporating:
- **Letter "Q"** - Clean, geometric interpretation
- **Quote marks** - Subtle reference to the product's core function
- **Conversation bubble** - Representing AI chat interface
- **Document/paper** - Simplified, abstract form
- **Checkmark/completion** - Symbolizing efficiency and completion

### Style Guidelines
- **Geometric & Clean:** Sharp, precise lines and shapes
- **Modern Minimalism:** Avoid complexity, embrace negative space
- **Scalable:** Must work from 16px favicon to billboard size
- **Versatile:** Effective in color and monochrome

### Logo Variations Needed
1. **Full Logo:** Symbol + wordmark
2. **Icon Only:** For app icons, favicons (square format)
3. **Wordmark Only:** For horizontal layouts
4. **Monochrome:** Black, white, and single-color versions

## Technical Requirements
- Vector format (SVG preferred)
- Minimum size: Readable at 16px height
- Clear space: Minimum 25% of logo height around all sides
- Backgrounds: Must work on white, dark navy (#1e293b), and colored backgrounds

## Usage Context
Logo will appear in:
- Web application navigation (top-left, 32px height)
- Marketing website hero sections
- Professional PDF documents (quotes/invoices)
- Email signatures
- Social media profiles
- Mobile app icons

## Current Placeholder
Currently using: Letter "Q" in white on a gradient blue square (`from-primary-600 to-primary-800`)

## Inspiration Keywords
Professional, intelligent, conversational, streamlined, efficient, modern, trustworthy, clean, sophisticated, business-focused, AI-powered

## What to Avoid
- Overly playful or casual designs
- Complex illustrations or detailed graphics
- Trendy effects that may date quickly
- Colors outside the specified palette
- Script or decorative fonts
- Generic office/business clich√©s (briefcases, ties, handshakes)
- Anything that looks like existing quote/invoice tools

## Success Criteria
The logo should:
1. Instantly communicate professionalism and trust
2. Hint at AI/intelligence without being obvious
3. Feel modern and forward-thinking
4. Scale beautifully across all sizes
5. Be memorable and distinctive
6. Work equally well in digital and print
7. Complement the overall design system (clean, modern, gradient-friendly)
